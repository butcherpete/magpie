Vimrc Configurations
--------------------

To ensure that Powerline fonts are displayed in MacVim (cf. `Fonts not
working in
MacVim <https://github.com/vim-airline/vim-airline/issues/719>`__.

.. code-block:: rest

   set guifont=Source\ Code\ Pro\ for\ Powerline:h16
